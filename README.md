## 💡 How to Use Arc

### Create Your Wallet
1. Open Arc
2. Tap "Nieuwe wallet aanmaken"
3. **Write down your 12 words** (critical!)
4. Verify 3 words
5. Done! 🎉

### Pay with Arc
1. Tap the **floating Zap button** or **Quick Pay card**
2. Select amount
3. Choose method:
   - **Lightning** - For crypto merchants
   - **Virtual Card** - For anywhere (via Coinbase/Crypto.com)
   - **Scan QR** - For merchant terminals
4. Confirm and pay!

### Multi-Chain
1. Tap your address at the top
2. Select network (Polygon = cheapest!)
3. All balances update automatically

## 🔐 Security

Arc is **non-custodial**:
- Your keys = Your crypto
- Private keys never leave your device
- Encrypted local storage
- No third-party access
- Full control always

### Best Practices
- ✅ Write recovery phrase on paper
- ✅ Never share private keys
- ✅ Test with small amounts first
- ✅ Use Sepolia testnet for testing
- ❌ Never screenshot your seed phrase
- ❌ Don't use unsecured WiFi for large amounts

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Balance not updating?**
→ Tap refresh button (↻)  
→ Check you're on correct network

**Transaction failed?**
→ Check balance + gas fees  
→ Try Polygon (cheaper)

**Can't scan QR?**
→ Allow camera access  
→ Ensure good lighting
